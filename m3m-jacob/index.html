About the Project

Where Jacob & Co's Precision Meets M3M's Excellence

M3M Jacob & Co. Residences marks the first-ever Jacob & Co. branded address in India — a creation so rare it places Noida on the world map of luxury living. Built as a collectible for the chosen few, it signifies more than architectural excellence; it represents a new benchmark for ultra-luxury living.

Rising in Sector 97, Noida, the development spans approx 6 acres, surrounded by approx 300 acres of open vistas with views of the Yamuna River and a golf course. It stands apart as the least dense part of Noida, designed for those who value space, privacy, and serenity — each residence offers a wrap-around balcony framing uninterrupted horizons.

Residences elevated approx 30 meters above ground level on the podium — a grand stage of life and leisure, with landscaped courtyards, art-lined promenades, mirrored water bodies, and sculptural lighting.
